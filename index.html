About Us | Our Mission

Discover the Movement Behind TRIMAX Skateboarding

Welcome to TRIMAX India’s first structured skateboarding academy built on passion, purpose, and progress. We go beyond just teaching tricks we shape mindsets, confidence, and community. From kids to adults, TRIMAX empowers individuals through certified coaching, global standards, and safe, inclusive training environments.

How TRIMAX Transforms
Beginners Into Confident Skaters

Intro Assessment

02

Foundational Training

Learn how to stand, move, turn, and stop safely—mastering foundational movements with proper gear.
