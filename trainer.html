we take pride in building a grassroots skateboarding culture that empowers children across India. Dr. Tribhuvan combines his medical expertise with sports coaching to ensure safe, inclusive, and holistic development for the next generation of skaters.

Work Process

How TRIMAX Transforms
Beginners Into Confident Skaters

01

Intro Assessment

We evaluate your age, confidence level, and balance basics to assign the right training level and coach.

02

Foundational Training

Learn how to stand, move, turn, and stop safely—mastering foundational movements with proper gear.

03

Skill Progression

Develop style, agility, and discipline through gradually progressing to tricks and flow.

04

Performance & Feedback

We track your performance, share insights with parents, and train you for competitions or advanced stages.
